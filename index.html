What is the low density lipoprotein calculator?
The LDL calculator is a tool that determines the amount of low density lipoproteins.
How to calculate low density lipoprotein?
To calculate low density lipoprotein you should use the following formula:
low density lipoprotein = total cholesterol - high density lipoproteins - 0.2 * triglycerides
